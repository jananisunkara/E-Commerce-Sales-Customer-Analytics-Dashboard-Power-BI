# E-Commerce Sales &amp; Customer Analytics Dashboard | Power BI  ## Overview  This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales and customer behavior using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product trends, and regional performance to support data-driven business decisions.
## Business Problem

Businesses require continuous monitoring of sales performance and customer trends to improve profitability and optimize strategies. This project aims to identify key revenue drivers, profitable segments, and sales trends across different regions and categories.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Interactive Visualizations
- Slicers and Filters

## Dashboard Features

- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Average Sales KPI
- Profit Margin KPI
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Segment
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Interactive Slicers

## Key Insights

- The Consumer segment contributed the highest sales revenue.
- Sales performance varied significantly across regions.
- A small number of products generated a large share of revenue.
- Profit margins differed across categories and customer segments.
- Monthly trends helped identify seasonal sales patterns.
